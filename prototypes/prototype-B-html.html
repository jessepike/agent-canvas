Key Findings

The enterprise AI market is led by OpenAI, with strong traction and developer momentum. Anthropic is a rapidly rising challenger driven by safety-first positioning and large procurement wins at Tier-1 banks.

Cohere and AI21 Labs occupy a credible niche around custom-tuned, on-premise deployments. Pricing power is bifurcating — usage-based at the top, tier-based in the middle, flat in the long tail.
