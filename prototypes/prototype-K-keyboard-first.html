AGF Positioning v3.0

cto· claude · [AGRC] 2 min ago

AGF is the secure AI governance platform for regulated enterprises. We unify policy, risk, and audit into a single system of record — built for scale, designed for trust.

Six Pillars

  • Governance that adapts to your risk profile
  • Policy automation with built-in controls
  • Evidence capture and audit readiness
  • Enterprise-grade security across markets and personas
  • Operator workflows with human-in-the-loop checkpoints
  • Open APIs for assurance, attestation, and supply-chain disclosure

Why now

Procurement teams at Tier-1 banks are rejecting AI proposals that cannot produce evidence of governed behavior on day one. AGF is the only platform where evidence is a side-effect of operating, not an artifact retrofitted later.
